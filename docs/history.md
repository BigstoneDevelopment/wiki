# History

The history of Bigstone  
[[TOC]]

## 2025:  
### June:  
Jun 19, 2025: The original Bigstone video by Daxis1 is published. [The video is linked here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0IJjAAtt9Z0)  
Jun 21, 2025: A datapack, to be named Bigstone Sandbox, began development. With this marked the beginning of a group known as Bigstone Development. The founder of said group, Huckle, is also the lead developer on the datapack.  
Jun 28, 2025: The original, now deprecated wiki was created.  
Jun 28, 2025: Gingr joined the Bigstone Development group as a wiki maintainer.  
Jun 28, 2025: Guigui joined the Bigstone Development group as the lead automation dev.  
Jun 28, 2025: Spin joined the Bigstone Development group as the lead wiki maintainer.  
### July:  
Jul 2, 2025: A resourcepack for Bigstone Sandbox was created.  
Jul 5, 2025: A follow up video was posted by Daxis1: [The video is linked here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OuDB1eWYT-g)  
Jul 7, 2025: Arttale joined the Bigstone Development group as a datapack dev and the lead resource pack artist.  
Jul 7, 2025: Xoliks joined the Bigstone Development group as a datapack dev, wiki maintainer, and resource pack artist.  
Jul 8, 2025: A Discord server by the name of Bigstone Development is created, and linked here: [Bigstone Development Discord](https://discord.gg/DuQuvVZGKH)  
Jul 9, 2025: The datapack was retired, and a new datapack under the same name began development.  
Jul 9, 2025: Chickon joined the Bigstone Development group as an automation dev.  
Jul 12, 2025: TheCrazy8 joined the Bigstone Development group as a wiki maintainer.  
### August:   
Aug 14, 2025: The original wiki is deprecated in favor of a new, more refined, and fancier wiki.  
### September:  
Sep 28, 2025: Daxis1 published the following video: [What if Redstone was REALISTIC?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jeukcDAFk_I)
